Introduct my favorite game




My favourite game is Tsuki's Odyssey.



Tsuki is the main character in Tsuki's Odyssey.

The background of Tsuki's Odyssey is talking about a rabbit named 'Tsuki' is leaves his work and back to his hometown and breaks.


Playing Method
There are also other characters
Players must complete the secret mission to unlock them.



Players can also choose to go fishing
and sell their catches to the shop owner to obtain carrots.



The player's daily task is to collect carrots to purchase furniture and decorate Tsuki's house.



When certain conditions are met, players can let Tsuki go on a trip.
Depending on different choices, Tsuki's travel methods and locations also vary.



This game is a very slow-paced one. However, when new plots or dialogues are unlocked, players will be very surprised.
